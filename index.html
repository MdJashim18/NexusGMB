Real Questions.
Straight Answers.

Google ranks local businesses based on three core factors: Relevance (does your profile match the search?), Distance (are you near the searcher?), and Prominence (do other sources confirm you're a credible business?). We optimize every layer — your GBP profile, review velocity, citation network, and post frequency — to maximize all three simultaneously. No shortcuts, no black-hat tactics. Just systematic authority building.
No long-term contracts. We operate month-to-month because our results should be the reason you stay — not a legal obligation. That said, local SEO is a compounding strategy: clients who commit to 3–6 months see dramatically stronger results than those who stop after 30 days.
Citations are online mentions of your business's Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) on directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages, Apple Maps, and Bing Places. When Google sees consistent NAP data across many trusted sites, it treats your business as more credible — which directly boosts your Map Pack ranking. Inconsistent citations are one of the most common reasons good businesses rank poorly.
Most clients see measurable ranking movement within 30–60 days and a noticeable increase in calls within 60–90 days. Competitive markets (lawyers, plumbers, HVAC in major metros) may take 90–120 days to hit top-3 positions. We provide a monthly performance report so you can track every milestone.
